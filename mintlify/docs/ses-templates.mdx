# SES Templates

Provisioned by `SesTemplatesStack`:
- **PetoWelcomeEmail** – used after signup (Auth API). Vars: `{{userName}}`.
- **PetoPasswordResetEmail** – used for reset flows (Auth). Vars: `{{resetLink}}`, `{{userName}}`.
- **PetoReminderNotificationEmail** – used by reminder processor. Vars: `{{petName}}`, `{{eventType}}`, `{{eventDate}}`, `{{notes}}`.
- **PetoEventNotificationEmail** (optional for future event notices). Vars similar to reminder.

Env vars used by Lambdas:
- `SES_FROM_EMAIL`
- `SES_WELCOME_TEMPLATE_NAME`
- `SES_RESET_TEMPLATE_NAME`
- `SES_REMINDER_TEMPLATE_NAME`

Permissions: relevant Lambdas get `ses:SendEmail`/`ses:SendTemplatedEmail` via their CDK stacks.
